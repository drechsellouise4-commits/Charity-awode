How are donations used?

Donations are used to fund the foundation's four core focus areas: Education: Funding scholarships, mentorship programs, digital literacy training, and vocational training for underprivileged students, first-generation graduates, and persons with disabilities.
Community Development: Supporting grassroots projects like providing clean water and healthcare in underserved communities. Empowerment: Implementing economic initiatives designed to strengthen local resilience. Global Impact: Extending the foundation's reach through strategic international partnerships, aligning efforts with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

How do you measure your impact?

The foundation measures its impact by tracking the outcomes and results of its programs within the four focus areas. This includes tracking the number of beneficiaries in educational and training programs and improvements in living standards in supported communities. Impact is assessed both qualitatively (through case studies and anecdotes from beneficiaries) and quantitatively (using numerical data).

Who manages the foundation?

The foundation is managed by a leadership team under the founder, Louis Awode. The team includes a Marketing Lead, Bolatito Ajao (Secretary); Event Strategist, Oyelola Olawale Kush; and Laide Soaga - Event Publicist. The founder himself is a first-generation university graduate with expertise in social entrepreneurship, technology, and advocacy.

What is the mission of the Louis Awode Foundation?

The mission of the Louis Awode Foundation (LAF) is to break down barriers and create opportunities for growth in communities, guided by the belief that access to education can change the course of a person's life, regardless of their background.

Does the foundation operate internationally?

Yes, a component of the foundation's strategy is "Global Impact," which aims to extend its reach beyond Nigeria through strategic partnerships with international organizations to address global issues like poverty and inequality, in alignment with the UN SDGs.

Where does the Foundation operates?

The Louis Awode Foundation operates as a global initiative with a presence in both Lagos, Nigeria, and Atlanta, Georgia, US.
